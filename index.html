About Quizzles

  • This quiz will consist of 10 multiple-choice questions.
  • Each question will have only one correct answer.
  • Participants will have 10 seconds to select their answer before the timer runs out.
  • Once a participant selects an answer, they cannot change it.
  • If the timer runs out before a participant selects an answer, their answer will be recorded as blank.
  • Each correct answer will be awarded 10 points.
  • There will be no negative marking for incorrect or blank answers.
  • The participant can see the hint for the answer, in such case 5 marks will be deducted.
  • The participant with the highest score at the end of the quiz will be declared the winner.
